# User Guide

## What is Meal Planner?

Meal Planner generates a personalised 7-day meal plan in seconds — lunch and dinner for every day of the week. No account needed. Just enter your preferences, get your plan, and start cooking.

The app is available in multiple languages. It automatically uses your browser's language setting.

---

## Generate a meal plan

1. Open the app.
2. Fill in the preferences form:
   - **Diet** — select your diet type (omnivore, vegetarian, vegan, etc.)
   - **Allergies / intolerances** — type any ingredients to avoid, or leave blank
   - **Number of people** — how many servings to plan for
   - **Max prep time** — the longest you want to spend cooking a single meal
   - **Weekly grocery budget** — an approximate budget range
3. Click **Generate my meal plan**.
4. Your 7-day plan appears with a lunch and dinner for each day, including the estimated prep time for each meal.

If you want to start over, click **← Modify my preferences** at the top of the plan.

---

## View a recipe

Click on any meal in the plan to open its full recipe. The recipe shows:

- Prep time and cook time
- Ingredient list with quantities (scaled to your number of people)
- Step-by-step instructions

Close the recipe panel to return to the meal plan.

---

## Replace a meal

If a suggested meal doesn't suit you:

1. Click **Replace this meal** under the dish you want to change.
2. A spinner appears on that card while a new suggestion is being generated. The rest of your plan stays exactly as it is.
3. Once the replacement arrives, only that meal is updated in the plan. All other meals are unchanged.

If the replacement fails (e.g. network issue), a short error message appears on the card. The original dish is kept. You can try again by clicking **Replace this meal** again.

---

## Usage limits

To keep the service free and fair, each IP address is limited to:

- **10 meal plan generations** per hour
- **20 recipe views** per hour
- **20 meal replacements** per hour

If you hit a limit, a message will tell you to try again later. The window resets after one hour.
